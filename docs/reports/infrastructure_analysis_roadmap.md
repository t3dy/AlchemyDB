# Report: Infrastructure Analysis & Roadmap

## 1. Current State Assessment
Our current infrastructure is built around a **Thematic/Symbolic** model of alchemy. We track "Topics" (Substances, Allegories, Apparatus) and "Practitioners" primarily as biographical entries.

**Successes:**
- Robust registry of 205 documents with AI descriptive summaries.
- Lexicon of 500+ terms linking practitioners to their primary texts.
- Functional relational viewer for entity mentions.

**Gaps:**
- **Flattened Role Logic**: We treat "Alchemist" as a monolithic category, missing the distinction between a *Court Projector* (Becher), an *Artisan* (material focus), and an *Academic Philosopher*.
- **Static Knowledge Types**: Our metadata captures *what* is being talked about, but not the *nature* of the knowledge (e.g., Artisanal vs. Textual vs. Political-Economic).
- **Missing Patronage Layer**: We lack a way to link alchemical projects to the economic and political goals of their patrons (Courts, Princes).
